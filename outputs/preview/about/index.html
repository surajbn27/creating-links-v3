About Creating Links

A way to redesign your life — dedicated to empowering children and supporting families.

Our Mission

At Creating Links, we believe that every child possesses a unique set of strengths that deserve to be nurtured through specialized, intensive intervention. Our mission is to bridge the gap between home and the structured school environment by focusing on the holistic needs of every child.

We are more than just a clinic — we are a multidisciplinary team of expert occupational therapists, speech-language pathologists, behavior intervention specialists, and special educators dedicated to your child's growth.

🌱

Individualized Growth

We use a comprehensive initial assessment to create a personalized Individualized Education Plan (IEP) tailored to your child's baseline in communication and social-emotional regulation.

📖

"Learning to Learn" Philosophy

Our primary goal is to establish the behaviors necessary for a child to succeed in any educational setting through a supportive, data-driven approach.

🚀

Pathways to Independence

From building foundational imitation skills to mastering personal safety and academic reasoning — we prepare children for full integration into mainstream school settings.

❤️

Functional Life Skills

We prioritize real-world independence by teaching self-care routines, emotional regulation tools, and visual schedules that empower children for life.

Why Choose Creating Links?

  • Experienced, certified therapists led by Dr. Bhuvanesh R
  • Individualized Education Plans (IEP) for every child
  • Data-driven progress tracking with evaluations every 3 months
  • Parent training, coaching, and support groups
  • Safe, sensory-friendly environment
  • Small 1:3 / 1:2 teaching ratio for personalized attention
  • Evidence-based communication tools including PECs and AAC
