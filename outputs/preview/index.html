Signature Program

School Readiness Program

Our structured program prepares children for mainstream schooling through three progressive levels — from building foundational imitation skills ("Wings to Fly") to achieving full academic and social readiness ("Sky's the Limit").

  • 1:3 / 1:2 teaching ratio for personalized attention
  • Data-driven monthly progress tracking
  • CCTV-monitored safe environment
  • Parent observation & regular feedback sessions
