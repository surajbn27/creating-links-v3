We believe in a collaborative approach where parents are actively involved in their child’s journey. Rather than fitting children into predefined boxes, we focus on understanding each child as an individual. Our goal is to build long-term functional independence, helping children develop skills that truly support their daily lives. We encourage both children and parents to grow and evolve, remaining open to diverse methods and opportunities rather than limiting ourselves to a single approach. As a team, we carefully evaluate every child, understand the parents’ concerns, and tailor our intervention to meet their unique needs
